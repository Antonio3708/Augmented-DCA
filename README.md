Designing and testing new Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA) strategies for beginners.

The goal is to integrate complex macroeconomic variables and market trends into simple, minimalistic strategies that anyone can understand and follow.

The instructions are as simple as follow:
“Buy X months’ worth of index funds upfront, then stop investing for X months when condition Y is triggered.”


The strategies aim to deliver higher performance not only compared to traditional DCA, but also relative to augmented DCA (ADCA) and modified versions of ADCA
